Q （qiū tiān lái le) 秋天来了 Autumn is coming
W (wǒ yòng ké bǎ zì jǐ bāo wéi qǐ lái) 我用壳把自己包起来 I surrounded myself with a shell
E (è de sè cǎi) 恶的色彩 the color of evil
R (róng huà dé hěn kuài ) 融化得很快 is melting fast
T (tā shuō yào zǒu màn yī diǎn) 他说要走慢一点 he said he would slow down
Y (yào xiǎng huí dào dì miàn) 要想回到地面 to get back to the ground
U (you) 你 you
I (i) 我 I
O (ò ，tān dé wú yàn de xīn) 喔，贪得无厌的心 oh, insatiable heart
P (péng zhàng lèi jī de yīn yǐng) 膨胀累积的阴影 the accumulated shadow of expansion
A (ài) 爱 love
S (suǒ yǒu de mèng kāi shǐ liáo cǎo) 所有的梦开始潦草 all the dreams began to become unclear
D (dà huǒ xí juàn mài tián)) 大火席卷麦田 the fire swept through the wheat fields
F (fǎn fù wú cháng de shuǐ mì táo) 反复无常的水蜜桃 the capricious peach
G (gāi sǐ de rǒng chǎng) 该死的冗长 is damn long
H (hái zài hū xī de huǎng) 还在呼吸的谎 the lie is still breathing
J (jiù huì fēi dào tiān shàng qù) 就会飞到天上去 will fly to the sky
K (kě y´ǐ zhī kàn tián de bù fèn) 可以只看甜的部分 can just look at the sweet part
L (lěng dàn jiāo ào guǎ yán) 冷淡 骄傲 寡言 cold, proud and speechless
Z (zhǎng dà le ) 长大了 grow up
X (xiàng jiàn shǐ bān ) 像箭矢般 It's like an arrow
C (cǐ kè) 此刻 at this moment
V (wéi shěn me) 为什么 why
B (biàn chéng māo biàn chéng lǎo hǔ) 变成猫变成老虎 become a cat, become a tiger
N (nì bì de yú) 溺毙的鱼 drowned fish
M (méi yÕu bēi de fén mù) 没有碑的坟墓 tombs without monuments
